Every feature built specifically for pool cleaning companies — not a generic tool with a pool icon slapped on.

Digital Chemical Log Per Pool

Record chlorine, pH, alkalinity, and stabilizer levels on your phone at every visit. Spotless tracks history per pool so you can see trends, catch problems early, and show clients exactly what was done.

🌞

Seasonal Service Scheduling

Batch-schedule all your spring openings and fall closings in minutes. Clients get automated reminders to book their slot, and the system prevents overbooking your crew on any given day.

Equipment Tracking & Service History

Log every pump, filter, heater, and salt system at each client’s property. Track maintenance dates, warranty info, and part numbers so you’re never guessing when something breaks down.

Route Optimization for Pool Rounds

Spotless groups nearby pools together and builds the most efficient route for your day. Add a new client and the system automatically finds the best slot. Save hours of drive time every week.

Generate timestamped, professional water test reports for every visit. Share them with clients instantly and keep a permanent digital archive for compliance and dispute resolution.

Automated Billing & Service Reports

Invoices go out automatically after each service with a detailed breakdown of what was done. Clients see exactly what chemicals were added and what was checked — building trust and reducing disputes.

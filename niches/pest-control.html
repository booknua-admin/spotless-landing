Follow-Up Visits Fall Through the Cracks Constantly

An inspector shows up and asks for your pesticide application records, safety data sheets, and applicator license copies. You know they’re somewhere — in a filing cabinet, a truck glovebox, and maybe your email. One missing document could mean a fine or a suspended license.

Warranty and Guarantee Tracking Is Completely Manual

A client calls claiming their termite treatment is still under your 2-year warranty. Was their initial service 18 months ago or 26 months ago? You’re digging through invoices trying to figure it out while the client gets increasingly frustrated on hold.

The Fix

Here’s How Spotless Fixes This

Every feature built specifically for pest control companies — not a generic tool with a bug icon slapped on.

Automated Follow-Up Scheduling

Set follow-up intervals when you create a service — 7-day check, 14-day bait station review, 30-day re-treatment. Spotless auto-schedules every follow-up and assigns it to the right tech. Nothing falls through the cracks.

Compliance Document Management

Store SDS sheets, application records, license copies, and inspection reports in one searchable system. Get alerts before certifications expire. When an inspector shows up, pull everything on your phone in 30 seconds.

Customer Safety Communication

After every treatment, clients automatically receive a detailed report: what was applied, safety precautions, re-entry times, and pet safety info. Proactive communication builds trust and prevents anxious calls and bad reviews.

Surge Capacity Management

When seasonal demand spikes, Spotless shows you exactly where you have capacity across your team. Prioritize urgent inspections, batch-schedule routine services, and see real-time availability so you never turn away revenue.

Warranty & Guarantee Tracking

Every service has warranty dates logged automatically. When a client calls, see their warranty status instantly — start date, end date, terms, and covered pests. Resolve warranty claims in seconds instead of hours.

Treatment History & Analytics

Full treatment history per property shows what was applied, when, and by whom. Spot recurring infestations, track product effectiveness, and make data-driven decisions about protocols and pricing.

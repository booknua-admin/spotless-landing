Large Properties Need Multi-Visit Plans You Can’t Track

That 3-acre estate needs mowing weekly, bed maintenance biweekly, tree trimming quarterly, and a full landscape refresh twice a year. You’re tracking all of this in your head — and when something gets missed, the client notices before you do.

The Fix

Here’s How Spotless Fixes This

Every feature built specifically for landscaping companies — not a generic tool with a leaf icon slapped on.

🌧

Weather-Aware Scheduling

Spotless monitors the forecast and automatically flags at-risk days. When rain hits, reschedule affected jobs with one click — clients get notified instantly and your crews see the updated route on their phone.

📦

Material Cost Tracking Per Job

Log materials used on every job — mulch, soil, pavers, plants. Spotless tracks costs against your estimate so you know your true profit per project. No more guessing if that install actually made you money.

🍂

Seasonal Service Templates

Build templates for each season and transition your entire client base with a few clicks. Mowing becomes leaf removal becomes snow plowing — automatically. Pricing, frequency, and crew assignments all update together.

🔧

Equipment Assignment & Tracking

Assign mowers, trimmers, blowers, and trailers to specific crews each day. Track maintenance schedules, hours of use, and current location. Know exactly where every piece of equipment is at all times.

📋

Property Maintenance Plans

Create comprehensive multi-service plans for every property. Weekly mowing, biweekly beds, quarterly pruning — all on one calendar. Spotless reminds you before each service is due so nothing gets missed.

📈

Crew Performance & Job Costing

See which crews are most efficient, which jobs are most profitable, and where you’re losing money. Make data-driven decisions about pricing, staffing, and which services to push.

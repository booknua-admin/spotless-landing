Truck Capacity Management Is Just “Hope It Fits”

You book four pickups on Truck A but the second job was bigger than expected. Now the truck is full at 11am, the next two clients are waiting, and you either need to make a dump run in the middle of the day or reschedule people who took the day off for you.

Disposal Site Routing Eats Into Your Profit Margins

Some items go to the landfill, electronics go to the recycler, and that couch can be donated. You’re driving to three different locations between pickups, burning fuel and time. One wrong disposal choice could also mean a compliance issue.

Commercial clients and eco-conscious homeowners are asking what percentage of their junk was recycled or donated vs. landfilled. You know you donate a lot — but you have zero data to prove it, and you’re losing contracts to competitors who do.

The Fix

Here’s How Spotless Fixes This

Every feature built specifically for junk removal companies — not a generic tool with a truck icon slapped on.

Photo-Based Load Estimation

Customers upload photos through your booking form. Spotless guides them to capture the right angles and helps you estimate truck load percentage with visual references. Set accurate pricing before you even leave the yard.

Truck Capacity Scheduling

See estimated load per truck for the entire day. Spotless tracks cumulative capacity so you know exactly when a truck will be full and needs a dump run. No more mid-day surprises or angry rescheduled clients.

Disposal Route Planning

Tag items by disposal type — landfill, recycling, donation, e-waste. Spotless groups pickups and dump stops into the most efficient route so you hit each disposal site once, not three times throughout the day.

Same-Day Availability Management

Your online booking shows real-time availability. Customers see open same-day slots and book instantly. You get notified immediately, the job slots into the optimized route, and the client gets confirmation in seconds.

Sustainability & Donation Reports

Track every item’s destination — landfill, recycler, or donation center. Generate professional sustainability reports for commercial clients showing exact diversion rates. Win more contracts with data that proves your impact.

Instant Quoting & Online Payments

Generate accurate quotes based on load size and item types. Clients approve and pay online before you arrive. No more awkward on-site price negotiations or chasing payments after the job is done.

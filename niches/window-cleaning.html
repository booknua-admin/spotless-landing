Sound Familiar?

The daily reality of running a window cleaning business.

If any of these keep you up at night, you’re not alone — and you’re not stuck.

Quoting Properties You Haven’t Seen Is Pure Guesswork

A client says “it’s a three-bed semi” and you quote based on that. Then you turn up and it’s a Victorian with 34 panes, leaded glass, and a conservatory round the back. You’re locked into a price that doesn’t cover the time.

Seasonal Demand Swings Make Revenue Unpredictable

Spring and summer your phone rings non-stop with new enquiries. By October, half your round pauses “until next year.” You have no system to manage the surge or retain clients through the quiet months.

Safety Compliance Is a Paper Nightmare

Risk assessments, rope access certifications, ladder inspections, insurance documents — they’re scattered across binders, email chains, and your van’s glove box. If HSE ever asks, you’re scrambling to prove compliance.

Round Scheduling Is an Impossible Jigsaw

Client A is every 4 weeks, Client B is every 6, Client C is every 8 but skips December. Your round sheet is a mess of scribbled notes and highlighter colours. Adding a new client means reshuffling the entire week.

Access Issues Waste Hours Every Single Week

You arrive at a property and the side gate is locked. Or the alarm code has changed. Or the dog is out. Nobody told you, there’s no note in your system, and now you’re standing in the rain calling a client who doesn’t pick up.

The Fix

Here’s How Spotless Fixes This

Every feature built specifically for window cleaning companies — not a generic tool with a cleaning label slapped on.

Property Profiling With Window Counts

Build a detailed profile for every property: number of windows, frame types, access requirements, and photos. Quote accurately before you ever visit, and never undercharge on a complex property again.

Seasonal Demand Management

Automatically prompt pausing clients to rebook when their season returns. Run “spring clean” campaigns to fill your round early. Smooth out revenue dips with pre-booked schedules months ahead.

Safety Checklist & Compliance

Digital risk assessments, equipment inspection logs, and certification tracking all in one place. Ladder checks, rope access records, and insurance documents are always audit-ready — no more paper chaos.

Smart Round Scheduling

Set each client’s frequency — 4-weekly, 6-weekly, 8-weekly — and Spotless builds your rounds automatically. Add new clients and the system slots them into the most efficient position on your route.

Access Instructions Per Property

Gate codes, alarm details, key safe locations, dog warnings, and parking notes — stored per property and visible to your team on their daily job list. No more wasted trips to locked properties.

Round Analytics & Revenue Tracking

See revenue per round, time per property, and client retention rates. Identify which areas are most profitable and where you’re losing money on travel time between scattered clients.

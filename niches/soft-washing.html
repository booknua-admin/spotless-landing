Sound Familiar?

The daily reality of running a soft washing business.

If any of these keep you up at night, you’re not alone — and you’re not stuck.

Chemical Mix Ratios Are Different for Every Single Surface

Render needs a different ratio than UPVC. Slate roofs need a different dwell time than concrete tiles. Your new hire doesn’t know this yet, and one wrong mix on a client’s expensive cladding could cost you thousands in damage claims.

Insurance and Liability Documentation Is a Scramble

A commercial client asks for your public liability cert, your chemical safety data sheets, and your risk assessment — all before they’ll confirm the booking. You know you have them somewhere, but finding the current versions takes an hour you don’t have.

Clients Don’t Believe You Did Anything Without Proof

Soft washing results can take 2–4 weeks to fully show as algae and biological matter dies off. Clients look at their roof the next day, see it still looks green, and demand their money back. Without before-and-after documentation, it’s your word against theirs.

Feast or Famine With No Way to Smooth Demand

March to September you’re booked solid. October hits and enquiries drop to nothing. You have no system to pre-book next season, no automated campaigns to reach last year’s clients, and no recurring revenue to carry you through winter.

Large Properties Need Multi-Day Scheduling That Doesn’t Exist

A farmhouse with outbuildings, a church complex, or a housing estate contract — these jobs span 2–3 days. Your calendar treats every job as a single slot, so you’re manually blocking days, hoping nothing else gets booked over them.

The Fix

Here’s How Spotless Fixes This

Every feature built specifically for soft washing companies — not a generic tool with a cleaning label slapped on.

Surface-Type Chemical Profiles

Build a library of mix ratios per surface type — render, UPVC, slate, concrete tile, painted surfaces. Your team sees the correct ratio, dwell time, and safety notes for every job before they mix a single batch.

Digital Insurance & Compliance Certificates

Upload your public liability, chemical safety data sheets, and risk assessments once. Share them with commercial clients via a branded link. Get alerts before anything expires so you’re never caught out.

Before & After Photo Documentation

Snap timestamped photos before treatment and at follow-up. Send clients a visual report showing the transformation over time. Disputes disappear when the evidence is dated and indisputable.

Seasonal Campaigns & Pre-Booking

Automatically reach out to last year’s clients when soft washing season approaches. “Your roof treatment is due for renewal” emails go out at the right time, filling your spring calendar before you even start advertising.

Multi-Day Job Scheduling

Block out consecutive days for large properties as a single job. Spotless prevents overbooking, tracks progress across days, and sends the client updates as each phase completes. No more manual calendar blocking.

Revenue & Chemical Cost Analytics

Track profit per job type, chemical usage costs, and seasonal revenue patterns. Know exactly which property types are most profitable and where your chemical spend is eating into margins.

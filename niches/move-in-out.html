⚠️ Sound Familiar?

Move-in/move-out cleaning is brutal on businesses that wing it.

You’re not just cleaning a property — you’re protecting someone’s deposit, your reputation, and a relationship with a property manager who has ten other cleaners on speed dial. One slip and you’re replaced.

⏰

Tenants and landlords expecting perfection on impossible timelines

The lease ends Friday. The new tenant moves in Saturday. You get called Thursday afternoon. There’s zero margin for error, and every missed corner becomes your fault — even when the timeline was never realistic to begin with.

📋

No standardized checklists leading to callbacks and disputes

Every cleaner does it differently. Some miss the oven. Others forget the window tracks. Without a locked-in checklist, you’re gambling that your team remembers everything — and losing money on free re-cleans when they don’t.

👤

Property managers dropping you for one missed detail

You’ve done 50 flawless cleans for this agency. One complaint about a dusty skirting board and suddenly they’re “trying someone new.” The relationship is only as strong as your last job — and you have no system to prove consistency.

💰

Quoting inconsistently because every property is different

A studio flat versus a four-bed house. Furnished versus unfurnished. Pet damage versus normal wear. You’re guessing prices on the spot, undercharging on the hard jobs and overcharging on the easy ones — then wondering why margins are so thin.

📷

No photo proof when tenants claim the clean wasn’t done properly

The tenant says the bathroom wasn’t cleaned. The landlord withholds the deposit. You know the job was done — but you can’t prove it. Without timestamped, room-by-room photo evidence, it’s your word against theirs. And you always lose.

Standardized Checklists

Photo Documentation

Instant Quoting Engine

Feed in the property size, number of rooms, furnished/unfurnished, and condition — get an accurate quote in seconds. No more guessing. No more leaving money on the table or scaring off clients with inflated prices.

Property Manager Portal

Give your property managers their own login where they can book cleans, view job status in real-time, download photo reports, and see their complete history. They’ll never need to chase you for updates again.

Automated Reminders

Automatic notifications to your team before every job — with the address, access instructions, checklist, and property-specific notes. Reminders to clients when a clean is scheduled, completed, and ready for inspection.

Dispute-Proof Records

Every job creates a complete audit trail: who cleaned, when, which checklist items were completed, and full photo evidence. If a deposit dispute goes to arbitration, you have everything you need in one click.

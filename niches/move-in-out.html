Sound Familiar?

The daily reality of running a move-in/move-out cleaning business.

If any of these keep you up at night, you’re not alone — and you’re not stuck.

🎲

One-Off Jobs Make Your Schedule Completely Unpredictable

Monday you’re fully booked with three end-of-tenancy cleans. Wednesday you have nothing. Friday someone calls needing a move-out clean “tomorrow morning.” You can’t plan staffing, can’t forecast revenue, and your cleaners never know if they’ll get a full week of work.

💨

Estate Agents and Landlords Demand Last-Minute Turnarounds

The letting agent calls at 4pm: “Tenants moved out this morning, viewings start tomorrow at 10am.” They expect miracles on zero notice and won’t hesitate to use someone else if you can’t deliver. But saying no means losing the relationship — and every future referral that comes with it.

📋

Detailed Checklists Required to Protect Tenant Deposits

Landlords and inventory clerks need documented proof that every surface, appliance, and fixture has been cleaned to move-in standard. Miss one item on the checklist — the inside of the oven, the window tracks, the extractor fan — and the tenant disputes the deposit deduction. Guess who gets blamed.

💰

Pricing Varies Wildly Based on Property Condition

A “standard” end-of-tenancy clean could take 3 hours or 8 hours depending on whether the tenants left it reasonably tidy or absolutely destroyed. You quote a flat rate, arrive to find grease-caked ovens and mouldy bathrooms, and end up working at a loss. But quoting too high means losing the job entirely.

🚫

No Repeat Business Pipeline — Every Month Starts From Zero

Unlike residential cleaning, your clients don’t come back every week. A tenant moves out, you clean the property, and that’s it. You have no system to follow up, no way to convert that landlord into a regular client, and no automated pipeline to turn a one-off job into ongoing work.

The Fix

Here’s How Spotless Fixes This

Every feature built specifically for move-in/move-out cleaning companies — not a generic tool with a cleaning label slapped on.

📅

On-Demand Scheduling With Live Availability

Spotless shows your real-time team availability so you can accept last-minute bookings with confidence. Estate agents can see open slots and book directly. No more phone tag — if there’s a gap, it gets filled automatically.

⭐

Priority Booking for Estate Agent Partners

Give your best referral partners — letting agents, property managers, landlords — a dedicated booking portal with priority scheduling. They see your availability, book instantly, and get guaranteed same-day or next-day turnarounds. Loyalty rewarded, relationships cemented.

📸

Condition Checklists With Photo Proof

Create detailed, customisable property condition checklists that cleaners complete with timestamped photos. Every oven interior, every window sill, every skirting board — documented and automatically compiled into a report you can send to the landlord or inventory clerk.

💲

Condition-Based Pricing Tiers

Set up tiered pricing based on property condition — standard, heavy, or deep clean. Send clients a simple condition assessment form with photo upload before the job. Spotless auto-generates an accurate quote based on what they submit. No more undercharging for nightmare properties.

🔄

Automated Follow-Up to Build Recurring Revenue

After every move-out clean, Spotless automatically follows up with the landlord offering ongoing services: regular property inspections, pre-viewing touch-ups, or recurring communal area cleaning. Turn one-off clients into a steady revenue stream without lifting a finger.

📈

Revenue Forecasting & Job Analytics

See your pipeline at a glance: upcoming jobs, revenue projections, busiest periods, and which estate agent partners generate the most work. Finally understand the patterns in your business so you can staff up before the rush and market during the lulls.

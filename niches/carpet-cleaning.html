Sound Familiar?

The daily reality of running a carpet cleaning business.

If any of these keep you up at night, you’re not alone — and you’re not stuck.

Room-Based Pricing Turns Every Quote Into a Maths Problem

A client says “three bedrooms, a hallway, and stairs.” But what size are the rooms? Is the hallway 3 feet or 15? Are the stairs straight or L-shaped? You’re doing mental gymnastics for every single enquiry, and half the time your price is wrong.

You Don’t Know What You’re Walking Into

The client said “a few stains.” You arrive to find red wine on wool carpet, pet urine soaked into the underlay, and candle wax on the landing. Without a proper pre-job assessment, you’re unprepared and underpriced for the work ahead.

Equipment Double-Booking Creates Job-Site Chaos

You’ve got two extraction units and three technicians. One unit is out for repair, and two jobs are booked at the same time needing the same machine. Nobody checked, and now a client is getting rescheduled at the last minute.

Drying Time Gaps Are Killing Your Schedule

You clean a house at 10am and the carpets need 4–6 hours to dry before furniture goes back. Your next job is at 2pm across town. But what if the first client calls with a problem during drying? Your schedule has invisible gaps that eat into your capacity.

Clients Never Rebook for Maintenance Cleans

You do a brilliant deep clean and the client is thrilled. Six months later, the carpets look terrible again. They call a competitor because they forgot about you. There’s no system reminding them it’s time for a maintenance clean.

The Fix

Here’s How Spotless Fixes This

Every feature built specifically for carpet cleaning companies — not a generic tool with a cleaning label slapped on.

Room & Area-Based Quoting

Clients select room types, approximate sizes, and extras like stairs or landings from your booking form. Spotless calculates an instant, accurate quote based on your pricing rules. No more mental maths or underquoting.

Pre-Job Assessment Forms

Send clients a quick form before the job: carpet type, stain types, photos of problem areas, and pet information. You arrive fully prepared with the right chemicals, equipment, and realistic time expectations.

Equipment Scheduling

Track which extraction units, wands, and drying fans are assigned to which jobs. Spotless prevents double-booking equipment and alerts you when machines are due for service or out for repair.

Smart Drying Gap Management

Spotless accounts for drying time when scheduling your day. It blocks out post-clean check windows, schedules nearby jobs during drying periods, and ensures your technicians are never left idle between cleans.

Automated Maintenance Reminders

Automatically email clients when their carpets are due for a maintenance clean — 3 months, 6 months, or 12 months after their last service. They rebook with one click, and your repeat revenue grows on autopilot.

Revenue & Service Analytics

See which services generate the most profit, average revenue per room, and client rebooking rates. Identify your most valuable job types and focus your marketing on what actually makes money.

Sound Familiar?

The hidden costs of “winging it” are killing your margins.

Deep cleaning is a specialist trade. So why are you still running your business with generalist tools — or no tools at all?

⏰

You spend more time quoting jobs than actually doing them

Every deep clean is different — carpet type, square footage, stain severity, furniture moving. You’re manually calculating every quote, and half the time clients ghost you before you even send it. Hours of your week, gone.

💸

No way to track equipment, chemicals, and supply costs per job

You know your truck-mount costs a fortune to run. You know those pre-treatment chemicals aren’t cheap. But can you tell exactly how much each job actually costs you in materials? Didn’t think so. You’re guessing at margins — and probably undercharging.

💰

Clients price‐shop you against basic cleaners because they don’t understand the value

You’re using hot water extraction, professional-grade enzymes, and years of expertise — but to the client, you’re just “a carpet cleaner.” Without a way to educate them on the difference, they compare your €250 quote to a €60 Groupon deal and pick the cheaper option.

📷

Before/after results aren’t documented, so you can’t prove ROI to clients

Your work speaks for itself — but only if someone’s standing in the room. Once the carpet dries and life moves on, clients forget just how dramatic the transformation was. No documentation means no referrals, no repeat bookings, and no leverage for premium pricing.

🌤️

Seasonal demand swings leave you feast‐or‐famine with no predictability

Spring and autumn you’re turning jobs away. January and July? Crickets. You have no system to smooth out the peaks and valleys, no recurring revenue to fall back on, and no way to forecast what next month looks like. Every quiet week feels like a crisis.
